Part 3: Deploying and Distributing Operators for Public Use

This section will show you how to package your Operator and publish it for others to use, contributing to the open source ecosystem that is essential to the Operator Framework with the help of case studies. Finally, the lessons from this book will be summarized in an FAQ-style breakdown before applying these lessons to real-world examples of application and system Operators.

  • Chapter 8, Preparing for Ongoing Maintenance of Your Operator
  • Chapter 9, Diving into FAQs and Future Trends
  • Chapter 10, Case Study for Optional Operators – the Prometheus Operator
  • Chapter 11, Case Study for Core Operator – Etcd Operator
